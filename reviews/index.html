Title Fast Bird Part Localization for Fine-Grained Categorization
Masked Reviewer ID: Assigned_Reviewer_1
Review:
Question 
Overall Rating Accept
Detailed Comments This work presents a CNN-based part localization approach with a single forward pass in the network, and demonstrates significant speedup over previous methods. The method is generalizable to unseen classes and can localize multiple part instances.

While quantitative results are discussed minimally in the submission, the work is very promising, and one expects this to be covered in greater detail in poster form or an extended publication. It would also be interesting to see results on other datasets besides CUB. I recommend acceptance.
Masked Reviewer ID: Assigned_Reviewer_2
Review:
Question 
Overall Rating Strong Accept
Detailed Comments This paper proposes a fast part localization method based on deep convolutional networks. It leverages the recent work from hypercolumn and PartRCNN, and build a pixel-wise part classifier. The feature input is the hyper-column feature vector which is the concatenation of all filter responses from all feature maps. It achieves comparable results as PartRCNN method and it is 2 orders of magnitude faster. I think it is a strong workshop paper and it would be better to show some quantitative numbers of how accurate the parts are located.
